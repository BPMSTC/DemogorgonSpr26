Where Coffee Meets Code

Brewing innovation, one cup at a time since 2018

Our Story

The Beginning

We were just five friends with a crazy idea: the best code is written over great coffee. In 2018, five college friends found themselves spending countless late nights at various coffee shops around Stevens Point, debugging code and building their first startup.

But something was always missing. Traditional coffee shops didn't understand the needs of developers: reliable power outlets at every seat, lightning-fast Wi-Fi, comfortable seating for long coding sessions, and most importantly, staff who understood that "just five more minutes" really meant "two more hours."

The Vision Takes Shape

After their startup successfully launched, the group had a realization: they wanted to create the space they had always wished existed. A place where developers, designers, data scientists, and tech enthusiasts could feel at home. Where the baristas understood the difference between Java the language and java the drink. Where the walls would feature rotating artwork from local digital artists and the menu items would have names like "The Debugger" and "Infinite Loop Latte."

Code & Brew was born from this vision‚Äînot just a coffee shop, but a community hub for the tech-minded. A third space between home and office where innovation happens over perfectly pulled espresso shots.

Our Mission

To create an inclusive, inspiring space where technology and coffee culture intersect, empowering our community to build, learn, and connect.

Community First

We believe great things happen when passionate people come together. From hosting weekly hackathons to mentoring aspiring developers, we're committed to fostering connections that last beyond a cup of coffee.

Learning & Growth

Education is at our core. We offer free coding workshops, host tech talks from industry professionals, and provide a judgment-free zone where asking "how does this work?" is always encouraged.

Quality & Sustainability

From our direct-trade, single-origin beans to our compostable cups, we're committed to sustainability. Great code deserves great coffee, and great coffee deserves a healthy planet.

Community Impact

Coding Bootcamp Scholarship

In 2022, we launched the Code & Brew Scholarship Program, providing full funding for three students annually to attend coding bootcamps. To date, all nine recipients have secured jobs in tech, with several becoming regular mentors at our weekly study sessions.

Saturday Code Club

Every Saturday morning, local high school students gather here for our free coding club. Led by volunteer developers from the community, we've taught over 200 students the fundamentals of programming, web development, and computational thinking.

Monthly Hackathons

Our 24-hour hackathons bring together developers, designers, and entrepreneurs to build solutions for local nonprofits. We've helped create websites, databases, and custom tools for 18 community organizations‚Äîall fueled by free coffee and pizza.

Job Board & Networking

Our community job board has connected over 150 developers with local and remote opportunities. Monthly networking events help job seekers practice technical interviews and get resume feedback from experienced professionals.
